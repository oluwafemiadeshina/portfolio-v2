Evote

Evote is an app that provides students with faster and cheaper means of participating in various university elections and polls.

Role

Research, wireframes, prototypes, UI Design, User flows, UI/UX Designer

Platform

Andriod & iOS

Duration

3rd April, 2022 to 21st April, 2022

Sector

Election

Project Overview

Background

Evote is a platform by which univerisity students can participate in their university elections or polls, without going to the voting locations. It allows students to cast their votes easily instead of waiting in line for hours, stay informed, know who are the candidates by reading their manesfeatoes, and help them make decisions on who to vote..

Role & Responsibilities

Conduction interviews, surveys, paper and digital wireframe, low & high fidelity prototying, conducting usability testing, acccounting for accessibility, iterating on designs.

Tools Used

Figma

Google Forms

Figjam

UXtweak

Validation

Problem

In university elections, voters turnouts are not high in almost every college. Although in some cases they have compulsory voting, participating in the elections is very frustrationg task due to the number of crowd at the election poll, or riots that occur during the election period. One of the main reasons that most students don’t want to participate is because there is only one way to vote: by going to the votting booth which takes a huge amount of time and effort to cast a vote..

Project Vision / Goal Statement

The goal

Design a mobile app that provide an easy and accesible way for college students to vote in their universities elections. Make the registration process as seamless as possible, prioritize good usability, privacy reassurance.

Project Vision / Goal Statement

User research: summary

I conducted user interviews, and user survey and based on the results from the research I started by identifying key problems in the current election process and carried out a UX audit to understand the pain points and areas of improvement in the live experience of the election platform.
